Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Here are some of the most common things we get asked.

What exactly is Lemly?

Lemly is a live commerce toolkit designed for personal shoppers and streamers. It provides a floating widget that works over any social media or chat app, allowing you to create product cards, manage orders, and receive payments instantly without interrupting your live stream.

Who is Lemly for?

Lemly is built for the modern entrepreneur who sells live. This includes personal shoppers streaming from retail stores, content creators unboxing products on TikTok or Instagram, and anyone using live video to run a commerce business.

How is this better than just using DMs and PayPal?

Lemly streamlines the entire chaotic process. Instead of managing dozens of direct messages, tracking who wants what, and sending individual payment links, Lemly professionalizes your workflow. It creates a seamless checkout experience for your customers and ensures you get paid instantly, eliminating payment friction and delays.

What do my customers need to do to pay?

Your customers get the simplest experience possible. They just click a single link you share, which takes them to a clean checkout page with their items already in the basket. They do not need to download an app or create an account, which significantly increases the likelihood of a completed purchase.

How does the instant payout work with the Lemly Card?

As soon as a customer's payment is successful, the funds are immediately credited to your Lemly account balance. This balance is directly linked to your Lemly Visa Card. You can then use the card to spend the money instantly‚Äîonline or in-store‚Äîwithout ever needing to wait for a bank transfer.
